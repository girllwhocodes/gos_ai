Documents Guidelines Consultation

Below are detailed information about which documents are required for admission to the university. In the guidelines section you can find useful tips on how to write a motivation letter or time management. You can consult with our graduates of the program to ask questions you are interested in.

Required list of documents

Undergraduate Scholarships in Australia

  • Have a Certificate of Education obtained in the State of Victoria (VCE) or a similar Austrian or international document.
  • Have the required level of grades in all subjects, knowledge of which is necessary for mastering the program, as well as pass all entrance exams, interviews and auditions.
  • Exam in PPT format - the total score is not lower than 577, not lower than 4.5 for the written test THE.
  • The exam in iBT format is a total score of at least 79, points in aspects: Writing: 21, Speaking: 18, Reading: 13, Listening: 13.

South Korean Government Scholarship Program for International Students

  • The completed application
  • Form is the NIIED Pledge form.
  • Motivation letter
  • 2 letters of recommendation
  • Certificate of medical examination
  • Certificate of full secondary education (for bachelor's degree)
  • Transcript of school grades (for bachelor's degree)

Anthony Morris Scholarship at Concord College

  • The Anthony Morris Scholarship Application Form (click here)
  • A personal statement describing the applicant’s objectives, career/university aspirations, extra-curricular activities and their level of community involvement
  • Reference letter from the current school Principal/Head Teacher
  • Evidence of academic potential
  • Certified copies of certificates and transcript
  • A recent passport size photograph

Guizhou Government Scholarships for Southeast Asia

  • Resume (CV) Form
  • Foreigner Physical Examination Form
  • Valid passport photocopy
  • Highest educational diploma photocopy
  • Transcripts for highest educational diploma
  • Study or Research Proposal (600 words for bachelor applicants and 1000 words for master or doctor applicants)
  • Previous Certificates of Awards or Achievements (if any)
